Greetings

My name is Evan Kurpiewski. I am a graduate student at UNCW attending with a focus on getting my master's degree in computer science. However, I am originally from Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania being born on January 31st, 1990. I have always found technology and computers fascinating. Growing up with video game systems becoming mainstream only heightened my fascination of technology. I excelled at math from a young age and also found art to be very enticing. I loved the art of video games, especially concept art. When I was in high school, I attended CMU for a summer program specifically to focus on how to code and produce art for video games, both of my favorite things. I was hooked. I decided this would be the focus of my life. Since then I have dedicated myself to learning about technology and especially software and also art as a hobby. Enjoy my portfolio website and email me if you have any questions.
